This is a little song I wrote intended to be in the style of Ramsey Lewis's The In Crowd (and some of his other similarly styled songs)[1].

I played all three parts on the keyboard (piano, bass, and drums) and recorded this on GarageBand. I find it to be a bit sloppy at times; for instance, the solo over the bridge that begins around 1:44. I also don't feel like I was really able to recreate a similar feel to that of Ramsey Lewis, where the listener is inspired to tap his/her foot or get up and dance. I guess I'll keep trying.

[1] I'm not sure why musicians have the tendency to call certain songs they write "little" (e.g. the first verse of Don't Worry Be Happy by Bobby McFerrin). But I have a similar inclination for certain songs I write. I think it comes from the fact that I did not spend much time on this "composition"; I think of this tune more as a platform through which I can explore another idea (in this case, creating a feel similar to Ramsey Lewis style jazz).
